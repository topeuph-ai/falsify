# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Falsification Engine project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience
level, identity, or background. We commit to acting and interacting
in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, inclusive, and
healthy community.

## Our standards

Behaviors that contribute to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community as a whole.
- Showing empathy and kindness toward other community members.

Behaviors that are not acceptable:

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or
  political attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information — physical or electronic
  addresses, for instance — without their explicit permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in
  a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull
requests, discussions, commit messages, review threads — and in any
public space where an individual is representing the project or its
community. Examples of representing the project include using an
official project email address, posting via an official social
media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online
or offline event.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior
may be reported by contacting the maintainer at
`hello@falsify.dev`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and
security of the reporter of any incident, and to act in good faith
when determining a proportional response.
